Summary

A tiny, lightweight, embeddable viewer/consumer for TAP.

Especially helpful when you need a concise status display summarizing many many tests. Less than 300 lines of pure POSIX shell with no dependencies, meant to be included into the test suite of any project that rolls its own TAP producers. Conforms to TAP version 14. Also includes tapdiffer, a TAP producer for pairs of load and check files in a regression-test suite.
