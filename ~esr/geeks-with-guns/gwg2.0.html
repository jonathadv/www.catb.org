Release 2.0: Northern Virginia, 5 Dec 1998

We held yet another Geeks With Guns at the NRA Headquarters range in Virginia in conjunction with my talk at NOVAlug, the Northern Virginia Linux User's Group. Bruce Benson did the actual organization and has been promising for months to send pictures. Email him to bug him about this...

The event was quite successful, with about 25 people including celebrity guest Guido van Rossum (author of Python). Besides the usual pistols, we has a couple of AR-15s -- civilan-legal, alas, no 3-shot bursts or full-auto rock'n'roll.
